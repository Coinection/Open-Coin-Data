## Description

The German coins have three different designs representing Germany's national symbols and unity: the €1 and €2 coins feature the Federal Eagle as Germany's national emblem, the 10, 20 and 50 cent coins depict the Brandenburg Gate as a symbol of German reunification, and the 1, 2 and 5 cent coins show an oak branch representing Germany's natural heritage.
