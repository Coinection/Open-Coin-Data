## Description

Each of the Slovenian euro coins have a unique design. The €2 and €1 coins depict famous Slovenians (the poet France Prešeren and writer Primož Trubar), the 50 cent coin features Mount Triglav (Slovenia's highest peak), the 20 cent coin displays two Lipizzaner horses and the 10 cent coin shows local architect Jože Plečnik's unrealised plan for the Slovenian Parliament. The 5, 2 and 1 cent show important symbols from Slovenia's heritage.
